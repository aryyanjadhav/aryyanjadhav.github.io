Neuromorphic Computing & SNN Benchmarking

PyTorchsnnTorchSNNPython

The Challenge: Standard Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs) consume significant power due to continuous dense computations, creating a bottleneck for edge-AI and aerospace applications.

The Solution: I developed a biologically inspired Spiking Neural Network (SNN) using Python, PyTorch, and snnTorch. To overcome the non-differentiable nature of binary spikes during training, I utilized surrogate gradient descent, enabling effective backpropagation through time (BPTT) within a deep architecture.

Key Technical Implementations:
• Dynamic Architecture: Implemented Leaky Integrate-and-Fire (LIF) neurons to process temporal data streams efficiently.
• Surrogate Gradients: Applied surrogate functions to smooth the spiking process, facilitating precise gradient-based learning.
• Sparsity Optimization: Tuned the network to maximize temporal sparsity, ensuring neurons remain inactive until significant events occur.

Results & Impact: The event-driven model delivered superior performance with a fraction of the energy cost:
• Accuracy: Achieved 98.33%, outperforming the static baseline of 97.39%.
• Energy Efficiency: Reduced theoretical energy consumption by 3.17x (2,477 nJ per inference compared to 7,864 nJ).
• Sparsity: Achieved 96.7% temporal sparsity, validating the approach for neuromorphic hardware and power-constrained environments.

Intellectual Property

Integrating IoT and Machine Learning Revolution: The driving force of Industry 4.0

Published: 19/08/2022IoTMachine LearningIndustry 4.0

Detailed Invention Description:
This patent describes a comprehensive framework for creating smart, adaptive, and automated industrial environments, forming the backbone of an Industry 4.0 system. The invention is centered on the synergistic integration of two key technologies: the Internet of Things (IoT) for data acquisition and Machine Learning (ML) for intelligent analysis and decision-making.

Core Problem Addressed:
The invention addresses the limitations of traditional industrial processes, which are often siloed, reactive, and manually intensive. In conventional manufacturing, data from machinery is either not collected or is not used effectively, leading to inefficient operations, unexpected downtime, and high maintenance costs.

The Patented Solution:
The system solves this by creating a closed-loop, intelligent system:
• IoT Data Acquisition: The framework's first layer consists of a network of IoT sensors and devices embedded within industrial machinery, production lines, and supply chain logistics. These sensors continuously collect and transmit vast amounts of real-time operational data (e.g., temperature, vibration, pressure, output, asset location).
• Machine Learning Core: This raw data is then fed into a central machine learning model. This model acts as the "brain" of the operation, trained to analyze complex data streams to:
    - Identify Anomalies: Detect subtle, non-obvious patterns that signal a deviation from normal operation.
    - Enable Predictive Maintenance: Forecast potential equipment failures before they occur, allowing maintenance to be scheduled proactively, thus eliminating costly downtime.
    - Optimize Processes: Recommend or automatically implement real-time adjustments to operational parameters (like machine speed or energy consumption) to maximize efficiency, quality, and output.

Results & Impact (Automated Driving Force):
The true novelty lies in using the outputs of the ML model to drive industrial action. Instead of just presenting data on a dashboard, the system is designed to trigger automated responses, such as dispatching maintenance alerts, re-routing supply chains, or adjusting robotic controls. This creates a fully autonomous or semi-autonomous "smart factory" environment that learns and adapts over time.
